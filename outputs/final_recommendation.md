# Final Recommendation

## Key Factors Associated with Monthly Sales

- Marketing spend
- Footfall
- Inventory availability
- Customer ratings

## Management Focus

Leadership should focus on maintaining inventory levels, improving customer experience, and allocating marketing budgets efficiently.

## Variables That Should Not Be Over-Interpreted

Variables with weak statistical significance should be interpreted cautiously.

## Recommended Business Actions

- Increase effective marketing activities.
- Improve inventory availability.
- Enhance customer experience.
- Allocate resources to higher-performing store categories.

## Risks and Limitations

- Regression identifies association, not causation.
- External factors may affect sales.
- Some variations cannot be fully explained by the model.

## Why Correlation Does Not Imply Causation

Regression shows relationships between variables but cannot prove that one variable directly causes changes in sales.
