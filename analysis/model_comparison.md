# Model Comparison

| Model | Variables Used | Business Usefulness |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|
| Simple Regression 1 | marketing_spend | Measures effect of marketing |
| Simple Regression 2 | footfall | Measures effect of customer traffic |
| Multiple Regression | Multiple predictors + dummy variables | Most useful for decision making |

## Limitations

- Models do not capture all external factors.
- Regression measures association rather than causation.
